ABOUT ME

I was born and rasied in Albuquerque, New Mexico and I can tell you my 505 pride beats true despite living in California for the last twelve years. Red or green? Definitely green. What do I order from Frontier? #8 AND #9. Dion's is the best. I am a full stack software developer and digital media producer and director. For the last decade, I sought to make my various clients' visions and stories come into fruition through video and photography, and am now looking to do the same in the tech industry through app and website development. The main driving force behind my career change (or I like to think of it as a merge), is recognizing the importance of technology and what a powerful tool development is to change people's lives. I graduated from Loyla Marymount University in LA in 2010 with a Bachelor's Degree in Film and TV Production. LMU instilled within me an innate need to help others and the importance of strong leadership skills. I created my own production company upon graduating and have since worked wiht a variety of individuals ranging from choreographers to fortune 500 Companies. This taught me discipline, time management, attention to detail, perseverence, and flexibility in order to maintian a successful business for so long. For years I had toyed with the idea of going to a coding bootcamp, but took the plunge after volunteering with an organization that teaches the elderly how to use tech. It was there that I realized the struggles and confusion technology creates for people and I wanted to help bridge that by learning more myself. I currently attend LEARN Academy, a premiere coding bootcamp in San Diego, where they continously push me to step out of my comfort zone and learn the skills I need to be a software developer.
