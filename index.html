ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Chrissie Akau. I was born and raised in Albuquerque, NM and have lived in Los Angeles, CA for the last fourteen years. I am a full stack software developer and digital media producer and director. For the last decade, I sought to make my various clients' visions and stories come into fruition through video and photography, and am now looking to do the same in the tech industry through app and website development.

For years I had toyed with the idea of going to a coding bootcamp, but finally decided to take the plunge after volunteering with an organization that teaches the elderly how to use tech. It was there that I realized the struggles and confusion technology creates for people, and I wanted to help bridge that gap by learning more myself. I currently attend LEARN Academy remotely, a premiere coding bootcamp in San Diego, where they continuously push me to step out of my comfort zone and teach me the skills I need to be a software developer.

When I'm not in front of a computer or behind a camera, you can find me reading, cuddling with my dogs, or outside mountain biking, hiking, or playing flag football, beach volleyball, soccer, or disc golf.
